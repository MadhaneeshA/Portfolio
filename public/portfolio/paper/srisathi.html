Ortho Mobi is an innovative orthotic leg analyzing system that revolutionizes orthotic care. Using EMG, flux, and pressure sensors, it accurately measures leg metrics with and without an orthosis, enabling informed recommendations by orthotists. The data is transmitted to the cloud for universal access through a web page, featuring insightful visualizations and a live animatic view of limb position. Powered by AWS Lambda and Amazon RDS, Ortho Mobi ensures efficient data processing and storage. With its advanced technology, Ortho Mobi enhances rehabilitation outcomes, empowers orthopedic professionals, and improves the lives of patients.
