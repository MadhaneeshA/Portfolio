Home automation using PLC involves automating various aspects of a home, such as lighting, security, temperature, and water pumping, through the use of sensors and Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs). Different modes can be implemented, such as Elderly mode, Security Emergency mode, and Eco mode, to cater to specific needs. The system also includes security features such as biometrics and automatic gates/doors. The objective is to provide safety, convenience, control, comfort, and energy savings.
