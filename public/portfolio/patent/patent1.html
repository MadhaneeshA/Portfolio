This invention proposes a system to monitor and automate the daily collection of municipal waste in cities using Industry 4.0 technologies, such as IoT and smart solutions. The system utilizes RFID tags, barcode readers, GPS, and a control unit to optimize waste collection routes based on real-time monitoring of bin fill levels. The goal is to improve the performance, flexibility, availability, and cost efficiency of waste collection processes, while reducing energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions. The proposed approach includes a cyber-physical garbage collection paradigm and AI-based optimization algorithms to develop and optimize waste collection routes.
