InnoHacks, a 24-hour hackathon organized by Innogeeks at Krishna Institute of Engineering and Technology College in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, witnessed our team's remarkable achievement. With a focus on AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things), we participated and emerged victorious, securing the esteemed 4th place. Our innovative implementation and utilization of AIoT technologies impressed the judges and showcased our proficiency in combining artificial intelligence with the Internet of Things. This recognition highlights our team's dedication, problem-solving skills, and ability to leverage cutting-edge technologies to create impactful solutions within a limited timeframe. Bluetooth module, Adurino NANO, Fingerprint Sensor, 16x1 LCD Module are the components that we used to create the idea into a project.
